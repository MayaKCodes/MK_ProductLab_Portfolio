Service Design Blueprinting to Identify Quick Wins

Maya used service design methodology to enable a US bank to enhance their digital customer experience. She conducted a current state assessment and used those insights to develop a target state service design blueprint. This blueprint enabled the team to identify user pain points at different service levels within the organziation. The blueprint was used to identify high-impact "quick wins" the client could implement to enable self-service, improve customer visibility, and achieve cost savings.
