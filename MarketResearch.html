Global Market Sizing and Analysis

Maya supported a global issuer in conducting in-depth research on 60 global markets across over 30 use cases to size the market opportunity for a recently developed payment product. She then analyzed the findings of the research and developed strategic business recommendations to inform the expansion of the product.

BIN Migration Analysis

Maya executed numerous client working sessions across 10 key focus areas to develop an impact assessment report and strategic roadmap to address potential BIN migration impacts for a US-based issuer. Based on market research and insights from business stakeholders Maya was able to make BIN strategy recommendations to optimize available BINs while minimizing costs.
